Classify whether the following user question can be answered from the analytics database.

The database has TWO data sources you must consider answerable when the question fits:

1) **shipments** — Historical USAID/SCMS freight shipment rows (~10k+). Fields include:
   country, shipment_mode (Air, Ocean, Truck, Air Charter), product_group (ARV, HRDT, ANTM, ACT, MRDT),
   vendor, delivery dates, freight_cost_usd, weight_kg, line_item_quantity, line_item_value, and related columns.

2) **extracted_documents** — User-uploaded freight invoices/documents that were **confirmed** by the user.
   Use `confirmed_by_user = 1` when the user means “my uploads”, “invoices I added”, “confirmed extractions”, or counts of uploaded/confirmed documents.
   Questions that only ask about **confirmed** uploaded invoices, counts of extractions, or fields stored on `extracted_documents` are **answerable**, not out of scope.

Questions about carbon footprint, emissions, temperatures, product formulas, private customer names unrelated to the dataset,
financial forecasts, or any topic clearly not represented in **shipments** or **extracted_documents** are out of scope.

Respond with ONLY valid JSON — no markdown, no explanation, no code fences:
If answerable: {"intent": "answerable"}
If out of scope: {"intent": "out_of_scope", "answer": "<1-2 sentences explaining what data IS available and why this question cannot be answered>"}
